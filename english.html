About the Project

A culinary journey that tells the story of Israel

🌿 Why Food?

Food is the universal language everyone understands. No matter where you come from, what language you speak, or what culture you belong to — everyone eats, everyone connects through flavors, and everyone knows that behind every dish there is a story.

We chose to create a digital cookbook because we wanted to show Israel in a different way — not through politics or news, but through our diverse and rich kitchen. When someone cooks Yemenite hamin or Iraqi kubbeh, they are not just making food — they are experiencing a piece of Israeli culture.

🌍 The Story of Israel

Israel is a unique country — a nation that absorbed Jewish communities from all over the world. Every community brought with it its own cuisine, recipes passed down through generations, and the flavors of home.

Yemenite recipes from Yemen, Moroccan from Morocco, Ethiopian from Ethiopia, Polish from Poland — all of these recipes met here in Israel and created the extraordinary culinary mosaic that is ours.

📖 About the Book

"Roots in the Plate" is a digital cookbook presenting traditional recipes from all Israeli communities. Every recipe is accompanied by a story about its origin, the culture it came from, and its significance.

This project was created by students from Gvanim School as part of the Teen Spirit program — a proud collaboration of young storytellers and food lovers.
